Water

Clean Water for Rural Kenya Villages

Kibera, Kenya 1,240 Donors 12 Days Remaining By HopeWave Team

Over 2 million people in rural Kenya currently lack access to safe, clean drinking water. The journey to the nearest water source often takes women and children more than four hours each day — hours stolen from education, work, and family life.

Our Clean Water Initiative is installing solar-powered water purification systems across 12 villages in the Kibera and Siaya regions. Each system serves up to 500 families, providing a reliable, gravity-fed supply of clean water 365 days a year.

What Your Donation Funds
  • Solar-powered borehole drilling and casing
  • UV purification and filtration units
  • Community storage tanks and distribution pipes
  • Training of 3 local technicians per village
  • 12-month maintenance and monitoring program
Expected Impact

Once complete, this project will provide clean water to over 6,000 families — roughly 30,000 people. School attendance rates in target areas are projected to increase by 40%, as children — especially girls — are freed from daily water collection duties.
