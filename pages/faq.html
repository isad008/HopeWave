Volunteering

Most of our volunteer roles require no special qualifications — only enthusiasm, reliability, and a commitment to our values. For specialized roles (healthcare, engineering, legal), relevant professional credentials are required. We have plenty of opportunities for everyone, from local event support to remote content creation.

Field volunteers are unpaid, though HopeWave covers accommodation, in-country transport, and a daily meal stipend for all field mission participants. Travel costs to the deployment country are the volunteer's responsibility, though we regularly partner with airlines for discounted rates for our volunteers.

Field missions range from 10 days to 3 months, depending on the project. We also have shorter local volunteer opportunities (single-day events, weekend programs) and ongoing remote roles with flexible time commitments of as little as 2–4 hours per week.
Impact & Transparency

Donors receive regular impact updates via email, including photos, field reports, and measurable outcomes from the projects they support. We publish a full Annual Impact Report, quarterly financial statements, and program-by-program breakdowns on our website. We are also GuideStar Platinum-rated and an ICFO member.

We currently have active programs in 48 countries across sub-Saharan Africa, South and Southeast Asia, Central America, and the Pacific Islands. Our headquarters is in New York City, with regional offices in Nairobi, Dhaka, Kathmandu, and Manila.

Major donors and corporate partners are welcome to arrange site visits with advance notice and coordination with our field teams. We organise two annual "Impact Journeys" open to all supporters — immersive trips that let you see our work firsthand. Contact us to learn about the next available dates.
