Who We Are

A Movement Built on Hope, Driven by Action

Founded in 2010, HopeWave began as a grassroots effort by a group of 12 friends who believed that collective generosity could change the world. Today, we are a globally recognized nonprofit with programs in 48 countries.

Our model is simple: radical transparency, community-led programs, and measurable outcomes. We hold ourselves accountable to the people we serve and the donors who trust us.
