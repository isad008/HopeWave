Donating
Yes. HopeWave is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN 47-2901847). All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent permitted by US law. An instant tax receipt is emailed to you the moment your donation is processed. UK donors receive Gift Aid confirmation; Canadian and Australian donors receive equivalent documentation.
We publish our full financial statements, independent auditor reports, and a real-time expense dashboard at hopewave.org/financials. Every transaction is categorised publicly. Our 2% overhead covers only essential operations: banking fees, security, and minimal administrative staff — all of whom are paid below market rate by personal choice.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfer. All payments are processed through Stripe with PCI DSS Level 1 compliance and 256-bit SSL encryption.

Impact & Transparency
Within 90 days of your first donation, you’ll receive a personalised impact update with photos, measurable outcomes, and GPS-tagged project locations. Monthly donors receive quarterly impact letters. All donors have access to a real-time dashboard showing exactly how your gift was deployed.
Yes. Every major project is independently verified by a third-party evaluator before and after completion. Our annual financial audit is conducted by Deloitte and published in full at hopewave.org/audit. Field outcomes are cross-checked by local partner organisations who operate independently of HopeWave.
We never let a partially funded campaign go unused. If a campaign doesn’t reach its goal, funds already raised are applied to the most critical phase of the project, or — with your permission — redirected to a related cause where they can do the most good immediately. We always notify donors of any reallocation.
Volunteering
Not necessarily. While some specialist field roles (engineering, medicine) require relevant qualifications, many of our most impactful volunteers bring general skills: communication, project management, languages, photography, and a willingness to learn. We provide comprehensive training for all accepted volunteers.
Field placements are typically 1–6 months. Remote roles can be as short as a few hours a week on an ongoing basis. We work with your schedule and never pressure volunteers to commit to more than they can genuinely give.
