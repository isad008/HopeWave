Who We Are

A Movement Built on Hope and Trust

Founded in 2010 by 12 friends who believed collective generosity could change the world, HopeWave has grown into a globally recognised nonprofit operating in 48 countries. Our model is built on radical transparency — 98 cents of every dollar donated reaches programs directly.

We don't just deliver aid. We build lasting systems — water infrastructure, schools, healthcare networks, and community capacity — that generate impact long after our teams move on.

Our Journey

14 Years of Growing Impact

2010
Founded in Brooklyn

12 friends pooled $4,800 to fund a single water well in rural Uganda. That well still flows today, serving 400 people.

2013
Registered 501(c)(3)

Formalised as a legally registered nonprofit with operations across 6 countries and our first full-time team of 8.

2016
GuideStar Platinum

Awarded Platinum status and Charity Navigator's 4-star rating — recognising exceptional financial health and transparency.

2019
$5 Million Raised

Crossed the $5M milestone with active programs in 24 countries and a 600+ volunteer network worldwide.

2023
$10M & 30 Countries

Expanded to 30 countries and launched the sector's first real-time donor impact dashboard.

2026
$15M · 48 Countries

1,200+ volunteers, 40,000+ donors, 12,400+ lives measurably changed. The mission has never felt more urgent — or more possible.
