Our Story

Growing Together Toward a More Just World

HopeWave started in 2010 as a small group of friends who believed that the earth's most pressing problems — poverty, lack of clean water, inequitable education — are solvable. Today, we are a global movement.

Clean Water Access

Solar-powered purification systems for remote villages across three continents.

Education Programs

Building schools and funding scholarships for children who need them most.

Healthcare Outreach

Mobile clinics reaching thousands of patients monthly in underserved regions.
